Executive Summary

A Complete Platform for
Modern Hospitality Operations

TOWNSHUB PMS is a multi-tenant, cloud SaaS platform that unifies hotel operations, guest experience, property management, and business intelligence into a single, beautifully designed interface.

What is TOWNSHUB PMS?

TOWNSHUB is an enterprise-grade Property Management System designed for hotels, vacation rentals, boutique properties, and property management companies. It replaces fragmented software stacks with one integrated platform — from front desk operations to automated marketing content generation.

Who is it for?

Hotel owners and managers seeking operational efficiency, boutique properties wanting a modern guest experience, property management companies handling multi-unit portfolios, and hospitality groups that need real-time reporting across locations.

The Problem We Solve

Hoteliers today juggle 5–8 disconnected tools: a PMS, a channel manager, a CRM, a maintenance app, an F&B system, a marketing tool, and a reporting dashboard. TOWNSHUB unifies all of them — saving time, reducing errors, and improving the guest experience.

Our Competitive Edge

Built-in intelligent guest concierge, staff automation, and content generation tools. A dual-mode platform covering both hotel operations and residential property management. QR-code guest experience layer and PWA mobile capabilities — no app store required.
